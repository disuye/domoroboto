About

Enthusiast owned & operated workshop based in southern Portugal. Our focus is 1980s, 1990s, and young-timer classic cars up the early 2000s.

You do the driving...
We do the rest.

Mechanical restoration, troubleshooting, repair and recommissioning. Rebuilt to original factory spec using nothing but OEM parts, or 'just get it running fo the weekend'... whatever the scope, we help enthusiast car owners keep their enthusiast cars on the road.

  • Interval servicing and pre-flight checks.
  • Post purchase / out of storage refresh.
  • Replacing, upgrading or custom manufacturing parts.

All European marques catered for but our specific niche is E-series BMWs (if you want to get really specific then our niche is E30 M3, E30 320is, E34 M5; S14 & S38 powered cars). We've dabbled with various Porsches, Mercedes Benz (and the 16V Cosworth), Volkswagen, Volvo, Audi, Lancia, and more recently a Series 1 Lotus Elise.

Cartel Automotive is not, however, a full restoration shop. We prefer to avoid bodywork, rust repair and paint – it's not our skill set plus our involvement with interior & upholstery extends to swapping out or repairing functional components. There are already many workshops in Portugal well established for nut & bolt restoration and we prefer to let the experts take on those types of projects.

Anyway! We will chat cars all day long, so feel free to drop us a line, ask us anything, or just to say hello.

Services

Too many cars? Not enough free time? No space? Cartel Automotive has got you sorted.

Pre-drive & interval maintenance, short/long-term storage and local delivery service under one simple monthly service fee.

Contact us for space availability.

Have enough space, or planning to build a bigger garage at your residence? We also provide consulting services: Liaising with architects to help them understand the intricacies of car enthusiast garage construction.

From task to photogenic lighting, flooring systems, sensor monitoring & automation, air & water purification, compressed air installation, properly spec'd accessibility from gate-to-garage (Algarve driveways are a sports car's nightmare), ramp & 2-post lift installation, tool storage and more...
